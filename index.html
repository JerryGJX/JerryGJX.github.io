Junxian Guo

Ph.D. Student at MIT

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at MIT EECS, advised by Prof. Song Han.

Previously, I received my B.Eng. in Computer Science from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (ACM Honors Class). During my undergraduate study, I was also an undergraduate researcher advised by Prof. Jingwen Leng.

My research interests lie in Efficient Algorithms and Systems for Machine Learning.
